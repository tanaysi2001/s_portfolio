About Me

My name is Sushmita Gouda, from Mugdur village in Siddapur Taluk, Uttara Kannada District. I have been working with India Post since 21st July 2021, with over 3 years and 8 months of experience.

I started my career as the Branch Post Master (BPM) at Anandpuram Post Office, and also handled post delivery duties in the beginning. I currently continue as a BPM, managing all postal operations at the branch.

My work involves handling mails, managing savings accounts, and delivering government schemes. I also help create financial awareness in rural areas by supporting people in accessing essential postal and banking services.

Recently, I led a Special Account Opening Drive in SC/ST areas, successfully opening 75 new accounts to promote financial inclusion.

I am proud to serve through India Post, helping connect and support communities with reliable postal and financial services.
